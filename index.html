What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the method of protecting a computer, network or hardware from hostile software and intrusion.
This can include protection from viruses, malware, adware and trojans etc.
Methods of cybersecurity can include antivirus softwares, firewalls and access control systems.

Cybersecurity is made up of many different areas, and we here at Honeybees will aim to help you make the best possible decisions.
